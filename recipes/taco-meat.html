Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground meat
  • .5 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tbsp garlic powder
  • 2 tsp ancho chili powder
  • .5 tsp cinnamon
  • 3.5 tsp tomato paste
  • 1.5 c beef broth
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 clove garlic

Recipe

  1. Add beef to medium pan, pressed flat. Sear for 3 minutes, flip, sear another 3 minutes
  2. Mash/cut beef into small pieces, let cook in its fat for 3 - 5 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Add spices, and allow to toast until fragrant.
  4. Add tomato paste, then beef broth, then bay leaves. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, simmer, and reduce 30 - 40 minutes until all the liquid is gone.
  5. Stir in finely diced garlic
