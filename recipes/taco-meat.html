Taco Meat

Description

Ingredients

  • Vegetable oil
  • 2 c rice
  • 2 c chicken stock
  • 1 c tomato sauce
  • 1.5 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • lime
  • cilantro

Recipe

  1. Use saute function on Instant Pot to heat oil and start toasting the rice.
  2. Stir in broth, tomato, salt, and garlic
  3. Add lid, cook for 12 minutes on low pressure, immediately release once the time is done.
  4. Serve with fresh lime and cilantro.
